Emerging of Threat Hunting

Openhunting.io project by Rizqy Rionaldy (Nalonal)

Welcome to openhunting.io!

I am Rizqy Rionaldy, the driving force behind the inception of openhunting.io. With over 5 years of experience in the field of cybersecurity, specializing in threat hunting, I have recognized the need for innovation and further research in developing techniques for effective threat hunting.

As a threat hunter, I often find myself bound by limited information that can be shared with the public due to the confidential nature of the companies I work for. To bridge this gap and share my knowledge with the community, I have created this website as a platform for sharing and documenting information that can be disclosed. The information I share includes insights, threat reports, and valuable threat hunting tools. My passion for open-source drives me to make all the information I provide accessible and transparent.

This website serves as a medium for knowledge exchange and collaboration in the cybersecurity community. I believe that by sharing our experiences and insights, we can collectively strengthen our defenses against emerging threats
